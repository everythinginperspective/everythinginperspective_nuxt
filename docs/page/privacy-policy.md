# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 5, 2026_

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Everything in Perspective collects, uses, and protects your information.

## What information we collect

**When you visit our site:**

- IP address, browser type, pages visited (via standard web analytics)
- No personal information is collected unless you choose to share it

**When you subscribe to our newsletter:**

- Email address (required)
- First name (optional)
- We use this solely to send you our newsletter and occasional updates

**When you contact us:**

- Email address and message content
- We keep your message confidential unless you give us permission to share it

## How we use your information

- **Email subscribers:** Deliver weekly essays and announcements
- **Analytics:** Understand which topics resonate and improve our content
- **Contacting you:** Respond to inquiries, topic suggestions, and submissions

We do NOT:

- Sell or share your data with third parties
- Use your email for marketing beyond our own newsletter
- Track you across other websites
- Use dark patterns or manipulative practices

## Third-party services

We use:

- **Analytics:** Anonymous, aggregated data only
- **Email service:** Industry-standard encrypted platform
- **Hosting:** GitHub Pages for secure, private infrastructure

All third parties are contractually bound to privacy standards.

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

- Session cookies for navigation functionality
- No tracking cookies for advertisements
- No third-party cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting core functionality.

## Your rights

You have the right to:

- Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
- Correction: Update or correct your information
- Deletion: Request we delete your email from our lists
- Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails at any time

To exercise these rights, [contact us](https://everythinginperspective.github.io/page/contact-us/).

## Data security

Your information is protected using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS). Our infrastructure is hosted on secure servers with automatic backups. We regularly audit our security practices.

## Retention

- **Newsletter subscribers:** Data retained until you unsubscribe
- **Contact form submissions:** Retained for 1 year or until resolved
- **Analytics:** Aggregated data retained indefinitely; personal identifiers deleted after 13 months

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. We'll notify subscribers of material changes via email. Your continued use of our site constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

## Questions?

Have concerns about your privacy or our practices? [Get in touch](https://everythinginperspective.github.io/page/contact-us/). We take privacy seriously and welcome your feedback.

_Everything in Perspective is committed to transparency and ethical data practices. We believe privacy is not a feature—it's a right._
